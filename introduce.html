About AASCHOOL

AASCHOOL is a high school that uses a boarding school system, has a Cambridge curriculum, provides complete facilities to support the needs of students in pursuing their education.

FACILITY

SMA aaschool is one of the well-known boarding schools in North Jakarta, which is facilitated by five bathrooms, a counseling guidance room, School health businesses, school cooperatives, sports rooms, laboratory rooms, parking lots and WiFi in every classroom.
